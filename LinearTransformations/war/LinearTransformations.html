Linear Transformations

Blue and green are lines basis vectors in the domain and their images in the codomain; the red lines represent a test vector and its image. Grab and move the head of the red vector on the left to apply the transformation to different vectors; grab and move the heads of the images of the basis vectors on the right to change the transformation.
The picture on the left represents the domain of a linear transformation, A:R2->R2. The picture on the right corresponds to the codomain of A. A is fully determined by its action on the basis vectors of its domain, represented by the blue and green vectors. A can be applied to any vector in R2 in the domain, producing the displayed pair of red vector in the domain and its red image in the codomain.
A is represented by a matrix with respect to the standard basis in R2; this and A(v) are recorded below the pictures.
